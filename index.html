GLUTEN-FREE CHOCOLATE CAKE


preparation time : 10 min

cook time : 30 min

servings:12 slices


  • 2 cups all purpose glutan-free flour
  • 11/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 13/4 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 squares unsweetened chocolate,melted and cooled
  • 11/4 cups cold water

  • Preheat the oven to 350
  • Grease two 9x1 1/2" round baking pans and lightly dust with flour
  • Mix the first 3 ingredients
  • Stir the butter in the blender for about 30 seconds
  • Add sugar and vanilla and stir until combined
  • Add eggs, one at a time, beating 1 minute after cach
  • Add the cooled chocolate
  • Alternately add dry ingredients and cold water Into a prepared pan
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until finished
  • Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from the pan
